Add `@altara/aerospace` — flight-instrument React components for drone ground stations, eVTOL, UAV simulators, and aerospace research. Includes the flagship `PrimaryFlightDisplay`, plus `HorizontalSituationIndicator`, `Altimeter`, `VerticalSpeedIndicator`, `AirspeedIndicator`, `EngineInstrumentCluster`, `RadioAltimeter`, `TerrainAwareness`, `TCASDisplay`, `AutopilotModeAnnunciator`, and `FuelGauge`. Every component supports `mockMode`, reads Altara design tokens, and consumes any `AltaraDataSource`.
